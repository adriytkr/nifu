<TheoremBox>
  **Mean Value Theorem:** Let $f$ be a function continuous on $[a,b]$ and differentiable on $(a,b)$. Then, there exists at least one $c \in (a,b)$ such that

  $$
  f'(c)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}
  $$
</TheoremBox>
