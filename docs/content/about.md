# About

## Philosophy — Intuition Over Memorization

By focusing on intuition, **harder topics becomes easier to master**. You don't copy, you build and understand what you are doing. And that is much more fun ❤️!

## Why I built this?

I wanted to share a new way of learning. I wanted to share a place where you can learn any topic and have fun at the same time.

While I promotes the idea that intuition is superior to memorization, I also share rigorous demonstrations of proofs.
